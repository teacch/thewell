Re-imagining Education by Empowering Educators

We focus on Teacher Development, Educational Development, and Community Development. These are the pillars of our work to create lasting change in Nigeria.  

Our range of programs, include capacity building, mini-grants, mentorship, advocacy, and knowledge distribution, all designed to empower teachers and students.

Our fellowship program offers teachers intensive training, mentorship, and the opportunity to implement capstone projects that transform their communities.

Brief Overview

We aim to bridge educational gaps by equipping teachers in low-income schools with the knowledge, skills, and resources they need to make a lasting impact.
